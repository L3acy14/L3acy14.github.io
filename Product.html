MY HOLY GRAIL LOOKS
Everyone has those few products that you know you’ll use forever. Today I’m running you through mine! I seriously can’t imagine not having each of these be apart of my routine on the daily. Make sure you tell me what your go-to products are down in the comments so I can try out yours as well!
1. It Cosmetics CC Cream This has been my absolute go-to face product for years. Whether I’m breaking out or having a clear skin day, this product has my back. It has buildable coverage and a glowy finish that gives my skin so much life. Plus, a little goes a long way, so the product will last you forever before you have to repurchase. Get yours here!
2. Mario Badescu Glycolic Acid Toner This is my favorite toner ever. I find that it pulls the dirtiest and gross makeup remnants off of my face. On top of that, this product is anti-aging, evens skin tone, and has exfoliating properties. This is always a saving grace during breakouts, so if you have once prone skin I highly recommend you give this product a try. Get yours here!
3. NuMe Dirty to Flirty Dry Shampoo My favorite shampoo without fail. I don’t know what it is about this dry shampoo, but whatever the formula is fantastic. The smallest amount touches up my hair effortlessly. Pro tip - if you know your hair will be dirty the next day, put some dry shampoo in it and don’t rub it in. Just sleep and when you wake up your hair will have absorbed a ton of the dry shampoo and then you can just rub the rest away. Does the trick every time. Get yours here!
4. Tarteist Pro Glow Palette Holy glow, I love this palette! It comes with six different highlight shades so there is something in there for every skin tone and every makeup look. My personal favorite in the palette is a stunner and, trust me, it lives up to its name. Even in low light, my highlight stands out. I’ll be so sad when I hit pan on that one! Get yours here!
5. Victoria's Secret Velvet Matte Lip Cream in Adored For sure my go-to favorite lipstick. This formula is silky smooth and dries almost totally matte without drying out my lips. I have naturally very dry lips, so that is huge for me. It’s long wearing and this color, in particular, is the perfect everyday color for me. Get yours here!
6. Wet Brush If you don’t have a wet brush… what are you DOING?! If you know one thing about me it’s that I love brushing my hair. I do it first thing when I wake up and probably three more times at a minimum for the rest of the day. And my wet brush has changed the game. It’s super gentle on wet hair and helps to stop breakage. Plus it pulls through tangles without damaging your hair or hurting. You have got to try this asap! Get yours here!
7. Garnier Micellar Water I feel like this is one of those products that everyone should have in their skincare routine. I’m still shocked when people tell me they haven’t tried it. This is my favorite product for pulling off my makeup and it does a great job. You only need a little bit to get a lot out of it. I highly recommend adding this product into your daily routine. Get yours here!
8. Not Your Mothers Double Take Dry FInish Texture Spray This is theeeee best product ever! I add this to my hair almost daily and it almost makes it look amazing. It adds volume and such pretty texture into my curls. It can revive three-day-old curls and make them look perfectly tousled. If you’re looking for those off duty model curls, try this product! Get yours here!
